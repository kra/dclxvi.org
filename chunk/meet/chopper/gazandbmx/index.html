Gazelle and BMX-Extendo Fork Choppers.

The easiest way to create a chopper is by lengthening a fork with a number of fork blades or pair of pipes to create what we call the Gazelle and BMX-Extendo forks, respectively. We produce a steady stream of them, because they are simple, elegant, and easy to crank out.
