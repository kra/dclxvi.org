Monotube Choppers.

A monotube chopper is created by extending the fork of a bicycle with a single tube, and sticking another fork on the end of it. When made well, these forks tend to be stronger than the BMX-extendo or gazelle forks. When not made well, they crack quickly, because they are also stiffer than BMX-extendos and gazelles.

The Handicapper.

A stroke of genius! Weld some square tubing to a fork, and just bolt a wheelchair wheel onto the end! Even better, bolt another on for a spare! Mags, built in guardrails, and no tires to flat!!
