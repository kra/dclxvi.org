Outgoing links.

The dmoz open directory page devoted to chopper bicycles is the best place to find chopper bicycle clubs on the web.

There are plenty of low-content web sites about custom bikes and lowriders that all look the same. Bike Rod & Kustom is one of the few excellent ones.

Critical Mass is a take-back-the-streets movement that has sprouted in cities across the world. There's a webring, a "worldwide hub", and a page devoted to our local ride in Portland.

Josh Merrrow taught a design class at El Puente, a public school in Brooklyn, which devoted a class to designing and building strange bicycles, and we're thrilled that they credit us as one of their inspirations. Josh has also published a must-see page of his various human-powered fiascos.

Some pretty pictures are in a Smithsonian magazine article about bizarre bicycles.

The International Human Powered Vehicle Association deals with human-powered vehicles in any form. They host races that don't have all of the stupid restrictions on vehicle design that other bike races have.

And finally there is the bicycle pr0n. Or, rather, there isn't much of it, which makes that which there is all the more precious. Thanks to the anonymous distributor of the cyclerotic pages.
