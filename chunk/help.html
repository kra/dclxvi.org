Help Wanted!

C.H.U.N.K. 666 needs your help.

We're constantly on the lookout for news of others who are pushing the envelope of what a bicycle can do and what the bicycle is for. Alone or with others, with a torch or with a poster or just with a word or deed, brazen or sneaky, legal or illegal, outwardly weird or stealthily normal in appearance, achieving success or failing dismally, we want to know about it all, and we want to let others know about it too. The children need us - we all know what they are being told, and they're going to believe it unless they learn otherwise. We can't just talk about ourselves all of the time, however - there is a niche for everyone, and people have to learn that.

In particular, we've been trying to learn about the Wild Mustangs in Austin, Texas for a long time.

We'd also like more distributors for the zine. Consignment with split profits, or bulk orders at a discount are both acceptable.
