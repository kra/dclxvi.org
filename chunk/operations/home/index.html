Local activities.

At times, we find ourselves a little too worn out to travel very far. Luckily, we are easily amused.

Whenever two or more cycles are together, a disorganized derby is likely to be formed. If our constant jostling for dominance doesn't start something, it is an easy matter to shout a few insults about another's riding skill and get chased around the toxic waste pool.

Paybacks can be hell, however.

Less social Chunkers amuse themselves by finding features of the landscape to conquer.

We're also happy to just relax around the grill.

Much time is also spent honing our skills for the Chunkathlon.
