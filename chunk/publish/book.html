The C.H.U.N.K. 666 Zine.

We are proud to present three zine titles to the public. Each one is photocopied, staple bound, and made with the same loving lack of design skill as seen on these web pages. All will provide valuable kindling after the Apocalypse, and mostly contain the material presented on this web site in a more portable format.

These zines are being distributed by Microcosm Publishing, and are obtainable through their catalog or Web order form.

They can also sometimes be found at Reading Frenzy in Portland, Quimby's in Chicago, and Bikesmith's in Seattle. And if not, each of these locations has an impressive rack of literature to spend time with, anyway.

Distributors, joint publishing ventures, etc. always sought, especially in far-off locales. Bulk orders welcome, send email.
